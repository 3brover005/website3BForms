What is 3B Forms?

Introducing 3B Forms v4: The Ultimate Salesforce Native Tool to Transform Your Data Collection. Effortlessly design forms, surveys, assessments, and secure e-sign documents directly within Salesforce. With a powerful visibility conditional engine, support for all standard and custom objects, parent-child relationships, and file submissions, our latest release promises unmatched reliability and scalability. Experience innovative features like form migration and custom extensions. Dive into a streamlined interface, leveraging the latest technology, ensuring an intuitive and efficient form-building experience. Revolutionize the way you gather and manage data with 3B Forms.


  • Child and Parent Related Objects
    Seamlessly map and manage intricate data hierarchies with our Parent and Child Relationships feature. Supporting unlimited levels, 3B Forms empowers you to capture complex data structures effortlessly, ensuring consistent and organized data collection. Dive deep into multi-level relationships without constraints, streamlining your Salesforce data management.
  • Attachments & Files
    Enhance data richness with our Attachments & Files feature. Any file submitted through 3B Forms is meticulously associated with its target object(s), ensuring your documents are always aligned with the right data points. Whether it's Attachments or Files, we've got you covered, streamlining your document management within Salesforce and ensuring seamless accessibility and organization.
  • e-Signatures
    Elevate your form's credibility with our integrated E-Signature functionality. With a simple action, form builders can incorporate an e-signature field, ensuring authentic and verified submissions. Whether you're closing deals, validating agreements, or confirming data accuracy, 3B Forms makes it effortless to capture digital signatures, fostering trust and compliance within your Salesforce environment.
  • Conditional Rule Engine
    Unlock dynamic form customization with our Conditional Rule Engine. Adopting an intuitive if-this-then-that approach, form builders can craft intricate conditions, controlling the visibility, requirement status, and accessibility of fields and sections. Beyond that, trigger specific alerts, error messages, or even automate form completion based on user inputs. This ensures an intelligent and responsive form experience, tailoring each interaction to the user's responses and needs, all while maintaining data integrity and precision within Salesforce.
  • Native Salesforce Support
    Optimize your Salesforce experience with 3B Forms’ Native Salesforce Support. Seamlessly integrate and utilize Salesforce-specific functionalities such as dependent picklists and multi-picklists with ease. Our robust support extends to all field and object types, ensuring that your form building and data collection are in perfect harmony with your Salesforce environment. Leverage the full spectrum of Salesforce's capabilities, delivering enhanced usability and ensuring a unified, efficient, and data-cohesive user experience from form creation through to submission and data utilization.
  • Distribution and Version Control
    Maintain clarity and oversight with 3B Forms' Distribution and Version Control. Every iteration of your form is meticulously tracked, documenting all changes and ensuring you always have access to its evolution. Distribution is versatile and inclusive: use Authenticated Digital Experiences for portal users, Guest User Digital Experiences for those outside the Salesforce ecosystem, and Salesforce Sites for a broader reach. For your internal team, embed forms directly, ensuring everyone is aligned with the latest version. With 3B Forms, you're not just building forms; you're orchestrating seamless, controlled, and efficient data collection journeys across varied user bases.
  • Merge Text Support
    Personalize and contextualize your forms with our Merge Text Support. Drawing inspiration from the familiar mail-merge concept, form builders can effortlessly use our intuitive syntax, like {{Contact.Account.FirstName}}, to dynamically pull data straight from Salesforce. This ensures your forms are always populated with the most relevant and up-to-date information, enhancing user experience by providing tailored interactions and reducing redundant data entry. With 3B Forms, creating context-aware and data-rich forms becomes second nature.
  • Translations
    Diversify your outreach with 3B Forms’ Translations and Language Support. Form builders have the power to define and support multiple languages, ensuring inclusivity for a global audience. Every element on the form can be translated, offering a localized experience for users from different linguistic backgrounds. Moreover, with the flexibility to switch the form language on the fly, adapting to your target audience's needs becomes effortless. With 3B Forms, bridge linguistic barriers and provide a universally engaging and intuitive form experience, catering to a worldwide user base.
